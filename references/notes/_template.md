### `MNEMONIC vd, vs1, vs2`

**1. 어셈블리 문법**
```
MNEMONIC vd, vs1, vs2
```

**2. 인코딩 비트필드**
```text
 31        26 25     21 20     16 15     11 10                0
+-----------+---------+---------+---------+-------------------+
|  opcode   |   vd    |   vs1   |   vs2   |   imm / unused    |
+-----------+---------+---------+---------+-------------------+
```

**3. 동작 (의사코드)**
```
for i in 0..VLEN:
    vd[i] = op(vs1[i], vs2[i])
```
